## Use Case
This app is designed for community members who are already self-organized and coordinating in real time.

It helps teams gather observations, prioritize what matters, and condense key updates into clear text for fast sharing.

## Before You Start
- Set your team conventions first: what to track, what to ignore, and who decides priority.
- Keep notes factual and time-specific.
- Use card types intentionally so map, filters, and summary output stay useful under pressure.

## Recommended Workflow
- Collect: capture observations in Dispatch as they come in.
- Verify: update or correct details as new information arrives.
- Publish: use Summary/Export or Alert text formatting for your outgoing updates.
