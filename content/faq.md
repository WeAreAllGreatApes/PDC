## Card Types
### What is the difference between Vehicle, Vehicle List, and Observer Car?
- Vehicle is for tracking one specific suspicious vehicle across multiple updates and locations.
- Vehicle List is for tracking multiple vehicles in one focused category (for example, "Black SUVs in area"), where each note can capture a different vehicle.
- Observer Car is for tracking the observer's location and movement when they are reporting from a vehicle.

### When should I use Observer Bike?
- Use Observer Bike when the observer is moving and reporting from a bike.
- This helps keep movement context separate from vehicle-subject tracking.

### Can I switch a card type later?
- Yes, but switching type can remove type-specific fields.
- If you're unsure, pick the type that matches the primary purpose of the card and avoid frequent switching.

## Location Tagging
### Why are there both card-level and note-level locations?
- Card-level location applies to all notes in that card by default.
- Note-level location is for updates that differ from the card-wide location.

### What does Center Map do?
- Center Map repositions the map to a selected location without dropping a pin.
- Use it when you want map context aligned before making edits.

### Why does Set Location have a two-step flow?
- The first step selects a candidate result.
- The second step confirms the exact location you want to apply.
- This helps prevent accidental location changes.

## Working Fast
### Should I use tutorials every session?
- No. Tutorials are best for onboarding, refreshers, or training.
- For live use, rely on your team's workflow and shortcuts.

### Where do I find keyboard shortcuts?
Use the Shortcuts button in the top bar or press Ctrl + Shift + /.

### Is my data shared externally?
- No, not by us.
- Core note/session data stays local to your device.
- Location lookups are proxied for geocoding, but not linked to your saved notes in an external app database.
